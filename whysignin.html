Why Sign In? Understanding Authentication in Cookery

Discover the benefits of signing in to Cookery and how it enhances your AI-powered cooking experience.

When you first use Cookery, you might wonder why we ask you to sign in. Is it really necessary? What do you get out of it? Let's dive into the reasons behind authentication and the benefits it brings to your cooking experience.

🔐 Secure Authentication

Signing in ensures your API keys and personal settings are securely stored and protected. Your credentials are encrypted using industry-standard encryption methods and never shared with third parties. This means your OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google API keys remain safe and private.

⚙️ Personalized Experience

When you sign in, Cookery remembers your preferences across sessions. This includes:

  • Your favorite AI provider and model settings
  • Custom provider configurations
  • Dietary preferences and restrictions
  • Favorite recipes and cooking styles

🔄 Sync Across Devices

Your settings and configurations sync seamlessly between your Raycast extension, web interface, and other platforms where you use Cookery. Start a recipe on your Mac, continue on your iPad, and finish on your phone – everything stays in sync.

📊 Usage Analytics

Authentication enables you to track your recipe generation history, view usage statistics, and get insights into your cooking patterns. Understand which cuisines you explore most, which ingredients you frequently use, and how Cookery helps you become a better cook.

🎯 Future Features

Signing in unlocks exciting upcoming features:

  • Recipe Collections: Save and organize your favorite recipes
  • Meal Planning: Plan your week with AI-powered suggestions
  • Collaborative Cooking: Share recipes with family and friends
  • Personalized Recommendations: Get suggestions based on your cooking history

🛡️ Privacy First

We respect your privacy above all else. Your data is stored securely and used only to enhance your Cookery experience. We never sell your personal information, and you have full control over your data at all times. You can delete your account and all associated data whenever you choose.

Getting Started

Signing in is quick and easy. Simply click the sign-in button in Cookery, choose your preferred authentication method, and you're ready to go. Your account is created instantly, and you can start enjoying all the benefits right away.

If you have any questions about authentication or privacy, don't hesitate to reach out to our support team or check our safety guidelines.
