Why Sign In?

Signing in is simply a way to support the project.

When you first use Cookery, you might wonder why we ask you to sign in. The honest answer is simple: signing in is just a way to support the project and help keep Cookery sustainable.

💚 Supporting the Project

Cookery is developed and maintained as a passion project. By signing in, you help support continued development, server costs, and the time invested in improving the AI-powered recipe generation features.

🔐 Secure Storage

When you do sign in, your API keys and personal settings are securely stored and protected. Your credentials are encrypted using industry-standard encryption methods and never shared with third parties. This means your OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google API keys remain safe and private.

⚙️ What You Get

While supporting the project is the primary reason, signing in does offer some practical benefits:

  • Your API keys are stored securely in the cloud
  • Settings sync across your devices
  • Access to future features as they're developed
  • Priority support when you need help

🔄 No Pressure

You can use Cookery without signing in – it's completely optional. The core functionality works perfectly fine without an account. Signing in is simply a way to show your support for the project if you find value in it.

See how to use Cookery without an account →

🛡️ Privacy First

We respect your privacy above all else. Your data is stored securely and used only to enhance your Cookery experience. We never sell your personal information, and you have full control over your data at all times. You can delete your account and all associated data whenever you choose.

Getting Started

If you'd like to support the project, signing in is quick and easy. Simply click the sign-in button in Cookery, choose your preferred authentication method, and you're ready to go. Your account is created instantly.

Thank you for considering supporting Cookery. Every user who signs in helps make this project more sustainable and allows us to continue improving it for everyone.
