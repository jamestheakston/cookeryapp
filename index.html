Installation Steps

Since our platform is in early development, you'll need to install the package using your computer:

1. Get Sideloadly Download and install the companion program Sideloadly onto your Mac or Windows PC.
Download Sideloadly
2. Connect iPhone Plug your iPhone into your computer using your connection cable, open Sideloadly, and drop the downloaded IPA file. Enter your Apple ID email and click start. Then when prompted, enter your Apple ID password. If prompted, tap ‘Trust this computer’ and enter your passcode.
