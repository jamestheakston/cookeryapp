Getting Started with Cookery

Search for Cookery in the Raycast Store and install it.

Step 1: Configure Your API Key

Once you've selected the command to Generate Recipes, you'll be prompted to configure the extension with your API key. Cookery supports multiple providers, so pick your preferred provider from the dropdown menu.

Step 2: Enter Your API Key

To use Cookery, you'll need an API key. You can get one from your AI provider's website. Enter your API key in the settings to activate the extension, then click continue.

Generating Recipes

Once configured, you can start generating delicious recipes with Cookery.

Step 1: Enter Your Prompt

Type what you'd like to cook or the ingredients you have. Be as specific or as general as you like!

Step 2: Select Cooking Style & Dietary Requirements

Choose your preferred cooking style from the available options. This helps tailor the recipes to your preferences. You can also specify any dietary requirements you have - select them from the dropdown to ensure the recipes meet your needs.

Step 3: Generate Your Recipe

Press Enter or click the generate button to create your recipe. Cookery will use AI to generate a detailed recipe based on your prompt.
