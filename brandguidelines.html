1. Logo Identity & Usage

Usage Rules

  • Clear Space: Maintain a minimum clear space around the logo equal to 25% of the icon's width.
  • Integrity: Do not stretch, rotate, or alter the icon.
  • Impersonation: Do not use our name or likeness to mislead, impersonate, or falsely imply affiliation.

4. Brand Voice

Transparent: Always state intent clearly. Explain why specific features exist.

Respectful: The user is in control. Keep the experience optional and low-friction.

Minimalist: "Less is more." Remove unnecessary clutter to focus on recipe generation.

Brand Identity & Trademark Notice

Cookery AI is a developing brand. While we are not currently trademarked, we are actively building our identity and community. These guidelines protect our brand from misuse, impersonation, and unauthorized commercial exploitation.

Do Not Impersonate: You may not use the Cookery AI name, logo, or likeness to create websites, applications, or social media accounts that impersonate or falsely suggest an official affiliation with Cookery AI.

Attribution: When referencing Cookery AI, use it as a descriptor for the service, not as part of your own product or brand name.

Restricted Uses: Any use that disparages our brand, misleads users, or attempts to capitalize on our goodwill without express permission is prohibited and may result in a request for removal or legal action.
