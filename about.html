My Story

1

The Problem

I noticed that many people struggle with meal planning and finding recipes that match their ingredients and preferences. The process was time-consuming and often frustrating.

2

The Solution

I envisioned an AI-powered assistant that could understand your needs, suggest recipes instantly, and adapt to your preferences. Cookery became that solution.

3

The Journey

After months of development, testing, and refinement, I launched Cookery as a Raycast extension. Today, I help thousands of users discover new recipes and enjoy cooking more than ever.
