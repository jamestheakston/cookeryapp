Install on iPhone

Final installation steps

1. Connect Your iPhone

Plug your iPhone into your computer using a USB cable. If prompted, tap "Trust this computer" on your iPhone and enter your passcode.

2. Open Sideloadly

Launch Sideloadly on your computer. You should see your iPhone appear in the device list.

3. Drop the IPA File

Drag and drop the Cookery.ipa file you downloaded into Sideloadly. Enter your Apple ID email when prompted.

4. Start Installation

Click the "Start" button in Sideloadly. Enter your Apple ID password when prompted. The installation will begin.

5. Trust the App

After installation, go to Settings > General > VPN & Device Management on your iPhone. Find Cookery in the list and tap "Trust".
