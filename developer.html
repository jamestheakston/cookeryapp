What's Coming

Our developer platform will include:

OAuth 2.0 Secure authentication flow for integrating Cookery accounts into your apps.
REST API Access recipes, collections, and user data through our comprehensive API.
SDKs Official SDKs for popular platforms to streamline integration.
Dashboard Manage your applications, API keys, and usage analytics.

Use Cases

Here are some ways developers can integrate Cookery into their applications:

Meal Planning Apps Import Cookery recipes directly into weekly meal plans and shopping lists.
Grocery Delivery Let users add ingredients from Cookery recipes to their delivery orders.
Social Platforms Enable users to share and discover Cookery recipes within your community.
Smart Kitchen Connect Cookery recipes to smart appliances for automated cooking guidance.
