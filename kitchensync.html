Kitchen Sync Roadmap

Adding Data Sync support to Cookery - a phased rollout to ensure perfection.

Why a Phased Rollout?

We want to make absolutely sure Kitchen Sync is perfect before releasing it worldwide. By rolling out gradually, we can gather feedback, fix issues, and ensure the best possible experience for all users.

July 2026 Dev

Develop Data Sync

Building the core data synchronization infrastructure.

August 2026 UK Release

Sync: UK Release

Initial rollout to UK users. Gather feedback and monitor performance.

Sept 2026 US Release

Sync: US Release

Expanded rollout to US users with lessons learned from the UK.

Dec 2026 Worldwide

Sync: Worldwide

Global availability. All users can now enjoy seamless synchronization.

What is Kitchen Sync?

Kitchen Sync is our upcoming data synchronization feature that will allow you to:

  • ✓ Sync recipes across all your devices
  • ✓ Access your recipe history from anywhere
  • ✓ Share recipes with family and friends
  • ✓ Backup your favorite recipes securely
